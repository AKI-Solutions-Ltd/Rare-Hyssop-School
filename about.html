About Us

Welcome To Rare Hyssop School

It is both a privilege and a pleasure to welcome you to Rare Hyssop Schools. Here, our goal is a simple one – to be the one of best schools, where students Aim, Build, and Shine in a Godly manner. The following systems of belief are crucial to achieving this goal. The first is the creation of an environment where all community members feel happy and safe. Our School is a fully inclusive community built on mutually respectful relationships. It is a community that students are proud of and want to belong to.

Secondly, is the offering of a broad and balanced, but demanding curriculum that allows for the personalisation of learning and ensures that every learner experiences success, no matter what their trail might be. A closer look and you will find that our school offers a wide range of opportunities, for students to achieve their dreams in life. Of course, this must be supported by superb teaching and learning and, here at Rare Hyssop Schools, our exceptional teachers and their purpose-driven approach ensure unsurpassed learning experiences for our students. I look forward to meeting each and every one of you and showing you what we have to offer your child.

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practise the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.
